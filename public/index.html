Welcome to The Fitness Center

The Fitness Center offers rental equipment for many activities and also offers various group exercise sessions and individual activities to its clientele. It serves a local community of 25,000 and has 1500 members. The center is currently having issues with double booked activities and overbooked sessions and which is not only becoming a nightmare for the center’s staff but is also a frustrating experience for its members.

The database will serve as a management system that will help track available group sessions and equipment, fitness center members, individual activities, and the number of reservations made by members.

Table Name Description
Members An entity that contains data about our members.
Activites An entity that contains activites that the fitness center offers.
Equipments An entity that contains the available equipment the fitness center has.
Reservations An entity that contains information about reservations made for either a session or equipment.
